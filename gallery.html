A Magpie

I love collecting trinkets. May as well describe them a little.

  1. A case filled with japanese "pennies". Cast from what I assume is aluminum, it feels funny to hold in my hand because it's so light.
  2. A tuning fork. I've never used it to tune anything, but it's fun to hit against everything.
  3. Some quartz crystals. I don't know about their healing properties. Maybe they can cure my ailments.
  4. Casio watch. I like the transparent strap, very Y2K vibes. But I'm scared it'll stain if I leave it out in the sun.
  5. A push to open sticker. I plan to stick it on the pull side of my house door.

But I'm willing to leave some of it to the imagination. And also my mouth hurts from trying to overexplain everything. You wouldn't get it anyway...
